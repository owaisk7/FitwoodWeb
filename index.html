ABOUT US

FitWood is a dance fitness company that focusses on making exercising fun and also helps clients make holistic changes to their lifestyle.

Meet The Team

 

Our Services

 

Dance

FitWood has Bollywood, Bhangra, Belly Dance, Freestyle and various other styles to make exercising fun!

Nutrition

With diet counselling and daily check-ins, Team FitWood ensures you eat right as per your body type to embrace healthy living.

Core Focused Routines

We are not just fun and play, with dance workouts focussing on the core, you will soon bid goodbye to the fat on your stomach.

Body Weight Workouts

Using your own body weight, special and unique workout routines are created to reach your ideal weight target

Wild Fitwood

This is a very intense dance routine, created by Team FitWood, involving animal movements to burn extra calories, inspired by nature!

Fun Weekend Activities

On weekends, FitWood Family from across the world engage in group outdoor activities like beach runs, kayaking, treks and much more

Holistic Living Talks

Sessions addressing health and wellness of the clients take place every month by FitWood Trainers.

Wellness Care

At FitWood, we believe in taking a holistic approach to fitness. Apart from keeping track of your physical fitness, we also make it a point to check on your mental wellbeing.

Weight Monitoring

Your weight goal is our goal! We regularly keep check of your progress and provide recommendations for dietary and lifestyle changes according to the needs of your body
